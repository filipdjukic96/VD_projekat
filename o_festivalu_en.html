FESTIVAL

The Filmchella Festival began its work for the first time in 1999 as a co-star of the Coachella Music Festival, and in the following decade it has grown into one of the most popular film festivals both on the American soil and in the whole world.



What has covered him all the past years is the fact that he was open to all world cinemas, and to all relevant authors, as well as films that will not be found on the bioscript repertoire. The importance of FEST is reflected in the fact that the Belgrade audience - with films by some of the greatest directors of today such as Teo Angelopulos, Bob Rafelson, Volker Schlendorf, David Kronenberg, Ken Rasel, Vim Venders, Kishlsky, Hercog ... the opportunity to meet for the first time at FEST. The first FEST started to be quite spectacular, with "Odyssey in the Universe" by Stanley Kubrick, "Naked in the Saddle" by Denis Hopper, the "Meš" by Robert Altman, the films by Erik Romer, Louis Bunuel, Zivojin Pavlović ... Although many, from nostalgia but rightly, consider the FEST's first edition to be unmatched, the truth is that in the coming years we would watch films that will remain recorded in the history of world cinema in FEST in the coming years. In the history of the festival, numerous great actors and directors of the festival were also included: Marcelo Mastrojani, Harvey Kaytel, Liv Ulman, Bernardo Bertolucci, Sofia Loren, Robert De Niro, Antonioni, Johnny Depp, Milos Forman, Katrin Danev, Vim Venders , Abaz Kjarostami, Hana Šigula, previously mentioned authors Rafelson, Schlendorf, Russell ... the list is very impressive, honorable and long. Since its inception, FEST has always had the main and several accompanying programs, whose quality often exceeded the name of "accompanying". Numerous publications, exhibitions, and symposiums held during the festival were also "accompanying". So far - FEST has had about four million viewers and nearly 4,000 significant world films - who had a Yugoslav / Serbian and often a regional premier on it. Although the first FEST was held more than 40 years ago, this year with the same energy continues its big cultural and educational task extending the film horizons to all fans of film art. 2015, FEST becomes a true competition festival with four competitive selections: the main competition program - international selection, the program Serbian Film - National Selection, the program Granice - subversive and controversial films and films from the region that compete for the Nebojsa Djukelić Prize. Another novelty is the introduction of the Belgrade Winner Award for outstanding contribution to film art, as well as the Belgrade Winner for the Past Creation.
