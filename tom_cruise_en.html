TOM CRUISE


Tom Cruise


Date of birth: 3. jul 1962.

Place of birth: Syracuse, New Yrok

Years active: 1981 -

Short bio:

Tom Cruise was born in Syracuse, (New York), from Thomas Tom Cruise Mapoter III and mother Mary Lee Fife. In his childhood, he often moved to the United States and Canada. He lived in Ottawa (Canada), Luivilu (Kentucky) (where he attended St. Xavier High School), Glen Ridge, New Jersey (where he attended Glen Ridge High School) and Wayne, New Jersey. Prior to engaging in acting, he attended a school for a Catholic priest. He first appeared in the film for the first time in 1981 in the performance of Frank Zefirelli "Love without End", and he played the first major role in 1983 in the film Risky of the Work of the director Paul Brikman. Later he plays in films such as: Top Gan, Color of Money, Rainman, Born July 4, Interview with a vampire, Impossible mission, Jerry Magweer and others. He was nominated for the Oscars three times as Best Actress for films: Born July 4, Jerry Magweiler and Magnolia.
